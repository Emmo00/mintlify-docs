## Instrument summary

| Instrument | Minimum buy | Payment cadence | Return type | Backed by |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **BizYield** | $10 | Monthly | Variable | Business revenue |
| **BizCredit** | $100 | Weekly | Fixed over a 12-week cycle | Managed loan portfolio |
| **BizBond** | $1,000 | Quarterly or at maturity | Fixed | Sovereign government instruments |

## BizYield terms

- Minimum buy: $10.
- Typical duration: 12 to 36 months.
- Return source: verified monthly business revenue.
- Payment currency: USDC or local stablecoins.

## BizCredit terms

- Minimum buy: $100 per share.
- Cycle length: 12 weeks.
- Return rate: 4% per quarter.
- Weekly payment: $8.67 per share.
- Principal return: full $100 at the end of the cycle.

## BizBond terms

- Minimum buy: $1,000.
- Return rate: 10% per annum.
- Payment cadence: quarterly or at maturity, depending on the series.
- Payment currency: USDC or local stablecoins.

## Worked examples

### BizYield example

If a business allocates 20% of monthly revenue to the BizYield pool and you own 1% of that pool, your payout equals 0.2% of the business's verified monthly revenue.

### BizCredit example

If you hold 5 BizCredit shares, you receive $43.35 per week for 12 weeks, then your $500 principal is returned at maturity.

### BizBond example

If you hold $1,000 in BizBond, the annual return is $100, usually delivered as four quarterly payments of $25 each when the series pays periodically.
